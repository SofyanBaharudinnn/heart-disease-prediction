Welcome to HeartGuard Support Portal

This application is designed to help you predict the risk of heart disease based on standard clinical parameters using advanced Machine Learning technology.

How to Use the Prediction Tool:
  1. Login / Register: Create a personalized account or sign in with your Google/GitHub credentials. Your prediction history is kept private.
  2. Fill out the Clinical Form: Go to the Predict menu and enter all 13 clinical variables (e.g., blood pressure, cholesterol, heart rate) exactly as documented in your clinical tests.
  3. Run Analysis: Click "Predict Now" to feed your parameters into our Random Forest classifier.
  4. Review & Download: Analyze your results, read personalized lifestyle advice, and export the comprehensive evaluation as a PDF report.
Need More Help?

If you encounter technical issues or errors, please contact our support desk via the form on the homepage or reach out directly to support@heartguard.com.

{% if current_lang == 'en' %}
Q: Is this prediction output a final medical diagnosis?

A: No. HeartGuard is an intelligent screening assistant. The prediction results are probabilities meant for early risk assessment. You must consult a licensed cardiologist or doctor for professional diagnostic tests (like echocardiograms or angiograms).

Q: How accurate is the prediction model?

A: Highly accurate. Our system runs on a Random Forest Classifier trained on clinical databases. The model achieves an average accuracy of over 85% tested with Multi-Holdout Validation. You can inspect the real-time metrics of the active model on our Dashboard page.

Q: What happens to my input data?

A: Your data is secure and confidential. We store your clinical entries in an encrypted database linked directly to your secure user account. We never share or sell patient records to insurance companies or third parties.

Q: How do I export my results to show to my doctor?

A: Via the History Page. If you are logged in, go to the History menu, view details of any past prediction, and click "Download PDF Report". You can print this document or show it to your physician on your phone.

{% if current_lang == 'en' %}Clinical Parameter Guide{% else %}Panduan Klinis Parameter Medis{% endif %}
{% if current_lang == 'en' %}

Below is a brief clinical explanation of the 13 input parameters utilized by the HeartGuard Random Forest algorithm to predict cardiovascular risk:

1. Age & Sex

Basic demographic metrics. Cardiovascular risk profiles generally increase with age, and baseline statistical thresholds differ between males and females.

2. Chest Pain Type (CP)

Categorized into four levels based on symptoms: Typical Angina, Atypical Angina, Non-Anginal Pain, or Asymptomatic (no pain but underlying ischemic risk).

3. Resting Blood Pressure (Trestbps)

The patient's blood pressure (systolic) measured in mmHg upon resting. Standard target is below 120 mmHg.

4. Serum Cholesterol (Chol)

Total serum cholesterol level in mg/dl. High cholesterol (> 200 mg/dl) is a critical precursor to plaque formation in arteries (atherosclerosis).

5. Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS)

Indicates if fasting blood sugar is greater than 120 mg/dl. Hyperglycemia is strongly linked to coronary arterial complications.

6. Resting ECG (RestECG)

Electrocardiogram results at rest. Detects normal states, ST-T wave abnormalities, or left ventricular hypertrophy signs.

7. Max Heart Rate Achieved (Thalach)

The maximum heart rate recorded during a physical stress test. Generally decreases with age and cardiac insufficiency.

8. ST Depression (Oldpeak)

Electrocardiograph ST segment depression induced by exercise relative to rest. Indicates degree of myocardial ischemia.

{% else %}

Berikut adalah penjelasan klinis singkat mengenai 13 parameter input yang digunakan oleh algoritma Random Forest HeartGuard untuk menganalisis risiko kardiovaskular:

1. Usia & Jenis Kelamin (Age & Sex)

Faktor demografi dasar. Risiko penyakit kardiovaskular cenderung meningkat seiring bertambahnya usia, dan terdapat perbedaan kerentanan serta gejala awal antara pria dan wanita.

2. Jenis Nyeri Dada (Chest Pain Type - CP)

Diklasifikasikan menjadi 4 tingkat: Angina Tipikal (nyeri dada khas jantung), Angina Atipikal, Nyeri Non-Anginal (bukan jantung), atau Asimptomatik (tanpa nyeri dada tetapi memiliki risiko iskemia).

3. Tekanan Darah Istirahat (Resting Blood Pressure - Trestbps)

Tekanan darah sistolik pasien dalam mmHg yang diukur saat beristirahat. Target ideal adalah di bawah 120 mmHg.

4. Kolesterol Serum (Cholesterol - Chol)

Kadar kolesterol total dalam darah (mg/dl). Kadar kolesterol tinggi (> 200 mg/dl) memicu pembentukan plak penyumbat pembuluh darah (aterosklerosis).

5. Gula Darah Puasa (Fasting Blood Sugar - FBS)

Menunjukkan apakah kadar gula darah puasa pasien > 120 mg/dl. Diabetes atau kadar gula tinggi sangat memicu kerusakan pembuluh darah koroner.

6. Elektrokardiografi Istirahat (Resting ECG - RestECG)

Hasil rekam jantung elektro saat istirahat. Mengindikasikan kondisi normal, kelainan gelombang ST-T, atau tanda hipertrofi bilik kiri jantung.

7. Denyut Jantung Maksimal (Max Heart Rate - Thalach)

Detak jantung tertinggi yang dicapai pasien saat uji beban fisik. Kapasitas maksimum ini cenderung menurun pada penderita penyakit jantung koroner.

8. Depresi ST (Oldpeak)

Penurunan segmen ST yang terdeteksi pada elektrokardiogram setelah olahraga dibandingkan saat istirahat. Mengukur tingkat keparahan kekurangan pasokan oksigen ke otot jantung (iskemia).
